For Impatient Web Users, an Eyeblink is just too long to wait

This isn't an article that's so much as intended for those in the internet industry, it reads more like it's for those who are not tech-savvy enough to know the lengths that the companies go to ensure their site speed is faster.

However this line below caught my eyes

People will visit a Web site less often if it is slower than a close competitor by more than 250 milliseconds.

Now they are ultra-demanding customers we are trying to serve.

Every cowboy-programmer should take note that an extra iteration here, unnecessary API call there and a block of HTML comments that should have been deleted, could easily take up that 250ms and affect your business.
