For about half a year to a year, the title of the blog was somehow like "mmhan's gone comical". You see, my dear reader, as the comic above would suggest, mmhan has been suffering the pitiful disease of ADD. (just jokin... Oh look! there's a squirrel..)

Anyway, this blog has undergone an unannounced hiatus and it has been covered with dust, spider webs and what nots. However, he just received a wake up call. A wake up call that calls him and say, "To Hell with your laziness and excueses, get back to do what you can do a little good. Serve your readers and serve 'em better!".

He thought, "Oh, hell... what's there to lose?", and the another voice answered, "Freaking lots of hours!!".

And then he fell back asleep. Zzzzz....

(I'm just thinking about bringing this blog back to life, and weighting my options. Motivation and time is nowhere to be found when it's much needed.)
