Although I mainly work on web development, I'm required do design work sometimes as well, including logo design, poster design, leaflet design and many other. Not only because I'm doing designs, but also because, I'm fascinated by Typography, I tend to collect a lot of free fonts as well. The following is the list of top 5 free fonts from my collection in alphabetical order and a font viewer called AMP Font Viewer used for my collection of fonts.

FF Nuvo (FF Unit Rounded Bold)

Registration at FontShop.com is required to download this free font.

Resources

A funny thing is now I realized that the resource of most of the fonts above is Smashing Magazine's Font Category, which is one of the best resources for free fonts out there. There are a few other sites as dafont.com and fawnt.com.

If you also have a lot of fonts lying around in your computer and you have a hard time managing them and launching Photoshop or other graphic editing software, you might wanna have a look at AMP Font Viewer.

AMP Font Viewer

AMP Font Viewer is a freeware for managing and previewing installed/non-installed fonts. I've tried using many font viewers and this has been my favorite because of its neat little feature to install font temporarily.

Please share me and everyone else if you do have other good tools and resources for fonts and typography.
